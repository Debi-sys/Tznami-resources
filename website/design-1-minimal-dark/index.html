Our Story

Five young people with a dream, building something extraordinary one keyboard at a time.

Born from Passion

Tznami started in a small workshop in Środa Wielkopolska, Poland. We're five young people who fell in love with the art of mechanical keyboards — the precision engineering, the satisfying sound, the feeling of typing on something truly special.

We decided to build keyboards that we ourselves would want to own. No mass production. No shortcuts. Every single unit is hand-assembled, hand-tested, and hand-tuned by our team. When you type on a Tznami, you're typing on something someone poured their heart into.

Our name means "wave" — a force of nature that starts small and becomes unstoppable. That's our vision: to create a wave in the keyboard world, one meticulously crafted board at a time.

Tznami HS65

From €199 / base configuration

The HS65 is our love letter to mechanical keyboard enthusiasts. A 65% hot-swap layout with gasket mounting for that perfect typing feel, paired with a programmable rotary encoder and OLED display. Housed in a CNC-machined aluminum case that feels as premium as it looks.

🔧 Hot-swap sockets — Change switches without soldering
🎯 Gasket mount — Silicone gaskets for a soft, cushioned typing feel
🎛️ Rotary encoder — Programmable knob for volume, scrolling, and more
📟 OLED display — Shows layer info, WPM, and custom animations
💻 QMK / VIA compatible — Fully programmable, open-source firmware
🏗️ CNC aluminum case — Precision-machined 6063 aluminum
🔌 USB-C — Detachable cable, universal connectivity
🎨 3 colorways — Charcoal Night, Dusk Blue, Sakura Pink
🔥

Hot-Swap Freedom

Swap switches in seconds. Try linear today, tactile tomorrow. No soldering needed — just pull and plug.

🎵

Gasket-Mounted Sound

Silicone gaskets isolate the PCB for a deeper, more satisfying sound profile and a cushioned typing experience.

🎛️

Smart Encoder

The programmable rotary encoder handles volume, zoom, scrolling, brush size — anything you can map to a knob.

📟

OLED Display

The built-in OLED screen shows your current layer, words-per-minute, or custom animations and logos.

⚙️

QMK / VIA

Full open-source firmware support. Remap every key, create macros, set up layers — the keyboard is yours to program.

🏆

Premium Build

CNC-machined aluminum, PBT keycaps, silicone dampening, brass weight plate. Built to last a lifetime.
