# Tznami Brand Guidelines

**Version**: 1.0  
**Last Updated**: 2024  
**Owner**: Design Lead

These guidelines govern all use of the Tznami brand identity in digital and print materials. When in doubt, ask the design lead before deviating from these standards.

---

## 1. Logo

### Versions Available

| Variant | Use Case | File |
|---------|---------|------|
| Primary (full color) | Default use on white/light backgrounds | `brand/logo/tznami-primary.svg` |
| Reversed (white) | On dark or colored backgrounds | `brand/logo/tznami-reversed.svg` |
| Monochrome (black) | Single-color print (receipts, embossing) | `brand/logo/tznami-mono-black.svg` |
| Monochrome (white) | White print on dark substrate | `brand/logo/tznami-mono-white.svg` |
| Icon only (wave mark) | App icons, favicons, embossing | `brand/logo/tznami-mark.svg` |

### Logo Usage Rules

✅ **Do:**
- Use the logo on clean, high-contrast backgrounds
- Maintain minimum clear space: equal to the cap height of the "T" in "TZNAMI" on all sides
- Scale proportionally — always maintain aspect ratio
- Use the reversed version on dark backgrounds (Tznami Navy, black, dark photos)

❌ **Do not:**
- Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
- Recolor the logo outside of the approved monochrome and full-color variants
- Place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a scrim/overlay
- Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the logo
- Use the full wordmark at sizes below 16px (use the wave mark instead)

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 80px wide minimum for wordmark; 24px for mark only
- Print: 20mm wide minimum for wordmark; 8mm for mark only

## 4. Voice & Tone

**Brand voice**: Expert but approachable. We know keyboards deeply and respect our community's knowledge — we never talk down to enthusiasts.

| Quality | What this means |
|---------|----------------|
| **Precise** | Use correct technical terminology. Hobbyists notice when you get it wrong. |
| **Enthusiastic** | We're genuinely excited about what we build. Let that come through. |
| **Honest** | Be transparent about timelines, QC issues, and limitations. The MK community values honesty above polish. |
| **Warm** | We're a small indie team. Our communications should feel human, not corporate. |

**Avoid**: Hyperbolic marketing language ("revolutionary," "game-changing," "the best keyboard ever"). Let the product speak.

**Use**: Specific, accurate descriptions ("FR4 plate for a crisper, more defined sound signature" vs. "premium sound").

## 5. Photography Style

See `marketing/product-photography/README.md` for full guidelines. Summary:
- **Lighting**: Soft, directional — one key light with a reflector fill
- **Background**: Off-white or Tznami Navy felt/textbook backdrop (no busy patterns)
- **Color grading**: Slightly desaturated, lifted blacks — a refined, editorial feel
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, keyboard at slight angle (15–30°), clean desk context
- **Avoid**: HDR looks, heavy vignettes, oversaturated colors, busy desk props
