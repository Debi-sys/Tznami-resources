## Warranty & QC Policy

All Tznami boards go through a documented QA checklist before packaging. We cover:

- **PCB defects** (dead keys, LED failures): Full replacement PCB shipped at no charge within 90 days of delivery
- **Case cosmetic defects** (scratches, anodize imperfections beyond normal variance): Replacement or store credit case-by-case
- **Firmware issues**: Free firmware updates via QMK/Via; support available in our Discord `#order-support` channel
- **What we don't cover**: Physical damage from use, modification-caused failures, third-party accessory incompatibility

Contact: **support@tznami.com** or open a ticket in Discord `#order-support`

Response time: Within 48 hours on business days.
