# Email & Community

This directory covers Tznami's email marketing and community engagement strategy — from list management and IC posts to GB announcements on Geekhack and Reddit.

---

## Email Marketing

### Mailchimp
**URL**: https://mailchimp.com  
**Free plan**: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month  
Use for: Launch announcements, GB live notifications, shipping updates, post-GB thank-you emails.

**Audience segments to maintain**:
- **All subscribers** (general)
- **Previous GB buyers** (high-value segment — market future GBs here first)
- **IC survey respondents** (interested in a specific board)
- **Unshipped orders** (operational updates only)

**Automated sequences to set up**:
1. Welcome email (immediate on signup): introduce Tznami, link to Discord, link to current IC/GB
2. GB launch notification (manual trigger): sent to IC survey segment
3. Shipping notification (manual trigger): per order, sent when tracking number assigned

### Kit (ConvertKit)
**URL**: https://kit.com  
Better than Mailchimp for creator-style emails and sequences. Tagging system is more flexible for segmentation. Recommended if the list grows beyond 2,000 subscribers.

## Discord Community Server

Structure your community Discord with:
```
📢 ANNOUNCEMENTS
  #announcements     ← IC/GB/shipping updates (read-only for members)
  #changelog         ← firmware updates, revision notes

💬 COMMUNITY
  #general
  #show-and-tell     ← members post their builds
  #feedback          ← product feedback and suggestions

🛠 SUPPORT
  #faq               ← pinned FAQ (update regularly)
  #order-support     ← direct order/shipping questions

🎨 BUILDS
  #switch-talk
  #keycap-talk
  #builds-gallery
```

**Bot recommendations**:
- **Carl-bot**: Role assignment, auto-mod, welcome messages
- **MEE6**: XP/levels for community engagement, auto-moderation
- **Ticket Tool**: Create private support tickets for order issues (keeps #order-support clean)

## Geekhack

**URL**: https://geekhack.org  
The traditional home for IC and GB posts in the mechanical keyboard community. Required presence for any serious GB.

**IC Post best practices**:
- Post in the "Interest Checks" subforum
- Thread title format: `[IC] Tznami KB65 — 65% Gasket Mount | [colorway names]`
- First post must include: overview, renders/photos, spec table, IC survey link, tentative timeline
- Bump thread with updates — don't let it go quiet more than 2 weeks during IC
- Respond personally to every question in the thread (not template responses)

**GB Post best practices**:
- Post in "Group Buys" subforum
- Thread title: `[GB] Tznami KB65 — [dates] | [vendor list]`
- Link back to the IC thread in the first post
- Update OP (original post) with current status; post regular updates as replies
- Final update: confirmation of all units shipped; close thread gracefully

## Reddit Community Engagement

See `../social-media/README.md` for the full Reddit strategy. Key community rules:
- Never delete or edit a negative comment — address it directly and transparently
- A public QC issue addressed well builds more trust than a perfect launch
- Never brigade or mass-downvote criticism
- Always reply to questions, even after the GB closes
