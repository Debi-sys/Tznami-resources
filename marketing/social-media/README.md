# Social Media

This directory covers Tznami's social media strategy, scheduling tools, and content planning for all platforms.

---

## Scheduling Tools

### Buffer
**URL**: https://buffer.com  
**Free plan**: 3 channels, 10 scheduled posts each  
Recommended for: Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn scheduling. Simple queue-based workflow — add posts, set times, Buffer publishes automatically.

Setup:
1. Connect Instagram Business, Twitter/X, and TikTok accounts
2. Set posting schedule: 3×/week standard, 1×/day during active GB
3. Use Buffer's AI assistant for caption suggestions (always edit before publishing)

### Later
**URL**: https://later.com  
Better visual calendar and Instagram grid planning than Buffer. Shows how upcoming posts will look on your profile grid — essential when doing colorway reveals where the grid layout matters aesthetically.

### Hootsuite
**URL**: https://hootsuite.com  
More powerful (and more expensive) — good if you need team collaboration features (multiple people approving posts). Overkill for a 5-person team unless one person manages all social.

## Platform Strategies

### Reddit (r/mechkeys, r/mechanicalkeyboards)
The highest-priority platform for keyboard IC and GB announcements. Rules vary by subreddit — always read the sidebar.

**r/mechkeys key rules**:
- IC posts allowed; label clearly as [IC]
- GB posts must link to an external GB page or vendor
- No pure marketing/ad posts without community value
- Best time to post: Tuesday–Thursday, 9am–12pm EST

**Tips for high-performing posts**:
- Lead with a high-quality photo (not a render — real photos perform better)
- Write a genuine caption that explains what makes the board interesting
- Respond to every comment in the first 2 hours — Reddit's algorithm rewards engagement
- Don't delete and repost; edit the original
- Flairs: use [GB], [IC], [PHOTOS], [VENDOR] correctly

### Instagram
Best for:
- Colorway reveals (carousel posts showing all colorway variants)
- Close-up detail shots (switch texture, USB port, logo engraving)
- Stories: countdown timers, polls ("which colorway?"), behind-the-scenes

**Hashtag strategy**: 15–20 hashtags; mix of high-volume (`#mechanicalkeyboard`, `#mkb`) and niche (`#custommechanicalkeyboard`, `#keebs`). Don't use banned tags.

**Posting frequency**: 4–5x per week during GB; 2–3x standard

### TikTok
Growing rapidly in the MK community. Content ideas:
- Sound comparison videos (linear vs. tactile vs. clicky)
- "Building the keyboard" POV assembly timelapse
- Typing test with trending audio
- "How much does this keyboard cost?" reveal
- Pack and ship day-in-the-life

**Optimal video length**: 15–30 seconds for max completion rate  
**Best posting time**: 7pm–10pm local timezone

### Canva Templates
Build a library of Canva templates for:
- New colorway announcement (portrait + landscape)
- GB live countdown
- Shipping update notification
- "Thank you" post-GB
- Sound test thumbnail

Share the Canva team folder with all team members who create social content.
