# Marketing

This directory covers all marketing operations for Tznami: social media management, product photography, community engagement, email marketing, and analytics. Marketing supports every product launch, group buy, and brand awareness initiative.

---

## Marketing Toolchain Overview

### Social Media
- **Buffer / Later**: Scheduling posts across Instagram, Twitter/X, Reddit
- **Canva**: Quick-turnaround social media graphics, story templates, countdown posts
- **CapCut / DaVinci Resolve**: Video editing for Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts

### Product Photography
- **Adobe Lightroom / Darktable**: RAW photo editing and color grading
- **Capture One**: Alternative professional RAW editor (better tethered shooting support)
- Keyboard-specific lighting and shot list templates in `product-photography/`

### Email & Community
- **Mailchimp / Kit (ConvertKit)**: Email list management and campaign automation
- **Discord**: Internal team comms + community server management
- **Geekhack / Reddit**: IC and GB forum presence

### Analytics
- **Google Analytics 4**: Website traffic, conversion tracking
- **Meta Business Suite**: Instagram/Facebook insights
- **Reddit Analytics**: Post engagement tracking
- **Google Search Console**: SEO and search visibility

## Marketing Calendar Overview

Marketing effort is organized around product lifecycle phases:

| Phase | Duration | Marketing Focus |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| IC (Interest Check) | 4–8 weeks | Community feedback, teaser photos, IC survey |
| Pre-GB | 2–4 weeks | Hype building, colorway reveals, vendor announcements |
| GB Live | 2–4 weeks | Daily/weekly updates, urgency, FAQ management |
| Production Updates | 8–24 weeks | Transparency updates, progress photos |
| Ship Week | 1–2 weeks | Unboxing previews, tracking info, excitement |
| Post-GB | Ongoing | Review encouragement, community photos, next IC |

## Platform Priorities

1. **Reddit (r/mechkeys, r/mk)** — Highest reach for IC/GB announcements; the MK community lives here
2. **Instagram** — Best for product photos and colorway reveals; connects to broader lifestyle audience
3. **Discord** — Deep community engagement; best for IC surveys and live Q&A
4. **TikTok** — Growing platform for sound tests and ASMR content; reach younger enthusiasts
5. **YouTube** — Long-form reviews, assembly videos, sound comparison content
6. **Email list** — Highest conversion rate; use for GB launch announcements to past customers
